A couple other basic things worth just knowing exist:

  • Your lens (the 50mm) is great for close-up, tighter shots — it's not going to fit a whole room in frame, so don't worry if something looks too zoomed in, that's just what this lens does
  • The screen flips around so you can see yourself, which is handy for solo filming
  • Get a decent SD card — cheap slow ones can cause the video to stutter or stop recording
