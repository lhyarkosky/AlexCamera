🏡 Real Estate Videos

smooth walkthroughs of a room or house

my notes from this video

  • Shows a few basic camera movements specifically for walking through a room
  • Big takeaway: moving slow and steady is what makes it look expensive/professional, moving fast makes it look cheap
  • Uses a gimbal (a little stabilizer handle) to keep the movement smooth — mentioned as pretty key for this style
  • Also touches on settings/gear specific to this kind of video

Simple stuff to remember:

  • This lens is pretty zoomed in, so it might feel hard to capture a whole room — a wider lens would help a lot for this specific thing if you ever want to invest in one
  • Filming during the day with natural light coming through windows generally looks best
  • Slow, steady movement through each room > fast panning around
